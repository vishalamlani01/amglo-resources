Who We Are

Founded in 1987, Amglo Resources is a global leader in the natural resources sector, specializing in the production, trading, and distribution of metals, minerals, and energy products. With operations in over 20 countries, we are committed to responsible sourcing and sustainable practices.

Our mission is to deliver value to our stakeholders while supporting the global transition to a low-carbon economy.
